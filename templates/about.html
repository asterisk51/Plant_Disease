About Us

Welcome To Krishil !

At Krishil, We Believe That Healthier Plants Lead To A Healthier Planet. Our Mission Is Simple: To Empower Farmers, Gardeners, And Plant Lovers With Smart Tools That Make Plant Care Easier, Faster, And More Effective.

Using AI-Powered Disease Detection And A Personalized Plant Health Workspace, Krishil Helps You Identify Plant Problems Early, Track Treatments, And Keep Your Greens Thriving.

How It Works?

Krishil works by allowing users to upload images of their crops, which are then analyzed using an AI-powered model trained on plant disease datasets. The system quickly identifies whether the plant is healthy or infected, provides the name of the disease, and suggests suitable treatments and preventive measures. This helps farmers and gardeners detect problems early, reduce crop loss, and maintain healthier plants.

“Our Motive Is To Provide Farmers And Plant Enthusiasts With An Efficient Tool To Detect Plant Diseases Early, Reduce Crop Loss, And Promote Sustainable Agriculture.”
